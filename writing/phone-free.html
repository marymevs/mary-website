Three months without a phone

Going phone-free for a season. What broke, what got better, and the moment it became the seed for Gist.

Outline

  1. The decision. Why I did it. What I was hoping to find. What I was hoping to leave behind.

  3. What got better. The unexpected wins. The pace of a day without notifications. What attention feels like when nothing is pulling on it.
